New To Camping!

For new campers, start by choosing a beginner-friendly campsite and book early. Pack essentials like a tent, sleeping bag, layered clothing, a first aid kit, and a flashlight. Set up your tent on flat ground, away from water, and follow fire safety rules. Cook simple meals with a camp stove, store food properly to avoid attracting wildlife, and always respect nature by cleaning up and following "Leave No Trace" principles. Stay aware of weather changes, and bring a map or GPS for navigation. Enjoy activities like hiking, stargazing, and exploring the outdoors!
